# Romantic and Victorian (Long 19th Century)

**Era slug:** `romantic_victorian_19th_century`

## Scope

Roughly 1780-1914. Romantic heroism, transcendentalism, nature writing, the Russian novel, late Victorian moralism, the rise of mass society.

## Sub-divisions

From [02_TAXONOMY.md](../02_TAXONOMY.md):

- Romantic heroism
- Transcendentalism
- Nature writing
- Solitude and self-reliance
- Beauty, desire, the sublime
- Russian moral novel
