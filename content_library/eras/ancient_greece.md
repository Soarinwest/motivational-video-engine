# Ancient Greece

**Era slug:** `ancient_greece`

## Scope

Archaic, classical, and Hellenistic Greece; roughly 800 BCE to 30 BCE. Homeric epic, tragedy, philosophy from the Pre-Socratics through the Hellenistic schools.

## Sub-divisions

From [02_TAXONOMY.md](../02_TAXONOMY.md):

- Homeric epic
- Greek tragedy
- Socratic philosophy
- Plato and Aristotle
- Cynicism, Epicureanism, early schools
