# Civic Republicanism

**Tradition slug:** `civic_republicanism`

## What this tradition is

Tradition holding that liberty is preserved by the active virtue of citizens, not by procedural rights alone. Roots in Rome and the Italian city-states; modern voice in figures like Theodore Roosevelt.
