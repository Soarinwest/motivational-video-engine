# Stoicism

**Tradition slug:** `stoicism`

## What this tradition is

Greco-Roman school holding that virtue is the only good, that emotions follow judgments, and that freedom lies in governing what is in your control. Founded by Zeno in Athens; reached its lasting form in the Roman period through Seneca, Epictetus, Marcus Aurelius.
