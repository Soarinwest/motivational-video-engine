# Aristotelianism

**Tradition slug:** `aristotelianism`

## What this tradition is

Tradition descending from Aristotle. Virtue as habituated excellence, the mean between extremes, eudaimonia (flourishing) as the human end. Recovered in medieval Europe through Aquinas and the Islamic Golden Age.
