# Utilitarianism

**Tradition slug:** `utilitarianism`

## What this tradition is

Enlightenment moral tradition (Bentham, Mill) holding that the right action is the one that maximizes well-being across affected persons. The project uses it as a counterpoint to virtue ethics, not as a primary frame.
