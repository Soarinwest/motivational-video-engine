# Existentialism

**Tradition slug:** `existentialism`

## What this tradition is

Modern tradition (Kierkegaard, Dostoevsky, Nietzsche, Camus, Sartre, de Beauvoir) facing meaning, freedom, suffering, and death without the consolation of an inherited frame. Closer in spirit to ancient tragedy than to systematic philosophy.
