# Greek Tragedy

**Tradition slug:** `greek_tragedy`

## What this tradition is

Dramatic tradition (Aeschylus, Sophocles, Euripides) staging the collision of human will, fate, and consequence. The form Shakespeare and Dostoevsky inherit. The project draws on it for stories where ambition and pride meet their cost.
