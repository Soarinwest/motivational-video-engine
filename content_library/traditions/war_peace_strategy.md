# War, Peace, and Strategy

**Tradition slug:** `war_peace_strategy`

## What this tradition is

Tradition spanning Homer, Thucydides, Sun Tzu, Musashi, Clausewitz, and the 20th-century war writers. War as the proving ground of character, the source of grief, and the test of political order. The project treats war seriously, never as spectacle.
