# Christian Wisdom

**Tradition slug:** `christian_wisdom`

## What this tradition is

Moral and spiritual tradition spanning the Hebrew Bible, the New Testament, the church fathers, monastic writers, Dante, the Reformation, and the modern Christian moralists. The project uses it as a wisdom corpus, not a confessional position.
