# Transcendentalism

**Tradition slug:** `transcendentalism`

## What this tradition is

American 19th-century movement (Emerson, Thoreau, Margaret Fuller) holding that the individual soul stands in direct relation to nature and the divine, without intermediaries. Source of much American nature writing and ecological consciousness.
