# Epicureanism

**Tradition slug:** `epicureanism`

## What this tradition is

Ancient school founded by Epicurus. Holds that pleasure (rightly understood as the absence of pain and tranquility of mind) is the highest good. Often misread as hedonism; closer in practice to a discipline of moderation and friendship.
