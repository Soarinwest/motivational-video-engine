## Warning: the popular pseudo-Aristotle quote

The widely-shared line **"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit"** is **not** a direct Aristotle quote. It is a Will Durant paraphrase from *The Story of Philosophy* (1926). Do not place it in `direct_quote`. Use Aristotle's actual public-domain wording instead, e.g. the entries below.
