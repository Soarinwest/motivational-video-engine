# Suffering

**Theme slug:** `suffering`

## What this theme is

What is borne, not what is avoided. The frame for everything from chronic illness to bereavement to the slow disappointments of a life.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``grief`` - suffering is the wider experience; grief is the specific form for loss.
