# Duty

**Theme slug:** `duty`

## What this theme is

What you owe - to those you have responsibility for, to the work in front of you, to the institutions that depend on you. Sober, not sentimental.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``honor_service`` - duty is the obligation; honor_service is the disposition of carrying it well.
- vs. ``self_command`` - duty looks outward; self_command looks inward.
