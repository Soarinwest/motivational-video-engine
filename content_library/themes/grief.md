# Grief

**Theme slug:** `grief`

## What this theme is

Loss carried over time. The theme that connects Homer to the homefront in 1945.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``mortality`` - grief is what survivors carry; mortality is your own end.
- vs. ``suffering`` - grief is a particular form of suffering, anchored to a lost relation.
