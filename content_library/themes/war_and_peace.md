# War and Peace

**Theme slug:** `war_and_peace`

## What this theme is

War honestly portrayed. Always with cost - to the dead, to the survivors, to the homefront. Never as spectacle.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``women_and_war`` - war_and_peace is the full theme; women_and_war is its specific frame for women as historical subjects.
