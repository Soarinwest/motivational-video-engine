# Usefulness and Competence

**Theme slug:** `usefulness_competence`

## What this theme is

The man who can do the thing. Work done well. The opposite of performance.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``duty`` - usefulness_competence is capability; duty is obligation.
- vs. ``honor_service`` - usefulness is the skill; honor_service is the disposition.
