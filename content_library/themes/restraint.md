# Restraint

**Theme slug:** `restraint`

## What this theme is

The discipline of not acting on every appetite, opinion, and grievance. The negative half of self-command.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``self_command`` - restraint is the negative act of holding back; self_command is the positive act of governing.
