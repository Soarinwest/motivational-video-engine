# Women and War

**Theme slug:** `women_and_war`

## What this theme is

Women as historical subjects of war - survivors, widows, refugees, nurses, mothers, partisans. Not backdrops for mens stories.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``homefront_rebuilding`` - women_and_war is the experience; homefront_rebuilding is the after.
