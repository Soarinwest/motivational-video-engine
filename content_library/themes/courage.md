# Courage

**Theme slug:** `courage`

## What this theme is

Acting rightly in the presence of fear. Not the absence of fear, not bravado.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``restraint`` - courage is acting; restraint is not acting.
- vs. ``honor_service`` - courage is the moment; honor_service is the habit.
