# Self-Command

**Theme slug:** `self_command`

## What this theme is

The positive act of governing your own attention, judgment, and action. The first kingdom is the mind.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``restraint`` - self_command is governing; restraint is holding back.
- vs. ``duty`` - self_command is ruling yourself; duty is answering to something beyond yourself.
