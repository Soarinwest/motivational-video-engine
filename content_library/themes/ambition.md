# Ambition

**Theme slug:** `ambition`

## What this theme is

The drive to rise, to win, to leave a mark. Source of achievement and source of ruin. The tragic theme.

## How it differs from neighboring themes

- vs. ``honor_service`` - ambition is the appetite; honor_service is its discipline.
- vs. ``power_corruption`` - ambition is the engine; power_corruption is the failure mode.
